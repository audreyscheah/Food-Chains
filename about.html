Our Mission

focused on educating people about the aquatic ecosystems of the Atlantic Ocean and how food chains function underwater.

We strive to offer a detailed look into both marine biology and the interactions within the Atlantic ocean food web; this website provides a free, accessible resource for anyone interested in learning more about ocean ecosystems, the science behind food chains, and the importance of marine conservation. In addition, we want to bring awareness to the importance of preserving the delicate balance of life beneath the waves by promoting solutions to rapidly growing issues such as climate change and pollution.

Whether through beach cleanups or charity donations, we believe that you can be a part of the solution too!
